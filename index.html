About Our FFXIV Services

Welcome, Warrior of Light! Our services are tailored for both new and experienced players in Final Fantasy XIV. Whether you're just starting or looking to master advanced content, our team of seasoned players is here to guide you on your journey through Eorzea.

Our Services

1. In-Game Coaching

Struggling with end-game content, savage or ultimate raids? Our team can help with:

  • Job Optimization: Our team members are experts at reading xivanalysis, and can tell you where you can improve.
  • Raid Guides: Our toolboxes, and guides adhere to the current meta stratagies, and our team can explain any points of confusion in a one on one tutoring session.
  • Leveling Assistance: Our team will tell you the best route of dungeons, or FATEs to do, on a level by level basis to get you toward your end goal of a maxed character.

2. Character Customization & Glamour Consultation

Appearance is everything in FFXIV, and we offer specialized guidance on character customization:

  • Glamour Consultations: We work hand in hand with Eorzeacollection to provide the most stylish fashion possible.
  • Character Re-designs: We know that sometimes the optimal character race for your job doesn't have good glamour, and we can solve that by recommending a better race.
  • Seasonal & Event Glamours: Our guides to seasonal events can help you bypass mogstation costs with ease.
